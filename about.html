Our Journey

2020 August

The Oakwood Diner officially opens its doors as a small, humble eatery dedicated to bringing classic burgers to a modern community.

2020 December

A local food blog's rave review of our signature burger brings in our first wave of loyal, dedicated customers.

2022 September

We expand our menu to include our now-famous hand-spun milkshakes and simple dessert classics, a response to overwhelming customer demand.

2023 April

Voted "Best Local Burger Joint" in a community-wide poll, a testament to our commitment to quality.

2024 July

We celebrate serving our 50,000th burger, a major milestone that proves our dedication is paying off.

2024 November

To meet growing demand, we double our staff and introduce an online ordering system, making it easier than ever to get your Oakwood favorites.

Present Year

We are now exploring opportunities to expand, ensuring we remain true to the quality and passion that made us a community hub.

Our Commitment to Quality

It all begins with our commitment to quality. We hand-select the finest, ethically-sourced ingredients from local farms to ensure a fresh, natural, and premium start for every single burger.

Our passion for quality extends to the craft. Each burger patty is meticulously handcrafted, seasoned with a signature blend of spices, and prepared with care to create the perfect texture and flavor.

The magic happens on the grill. We sear each patty on a hot cast iron grill to create a rich, caramelized crust, locking in all the natural juices and flavors for an unforgettable, mouth-watering experience.

The final art. Every burger is thoughtfully assembled with crisp vegetables and our house-made sauces on a toasted, brioche-style bun, a final touch that brings all our flavors and textures together in perfect harmony.
